Investing in Your Child’s Future

At Felicity, we understand the importance of securing a bright future for the next generation. That’s why we've created the Felicity Junior Fund, a unique investment opportunity in Zambia tailored specifically for parents who want to invest in their child's financial well-being. This specialized fund is designed to grow your child’s wealth steadily over time, allowing you to build a financial foundation they can rely on when they reach the age of 16—or when they are ready to take control of their finances.

Why Choose the Felicity Junior Fund?

Investing early in your child’s future offers a unique opportunity to harness the power of long-term growth. The earlier you start, the more time your investment has to mature, providing your child with a robust financial base as they enter adulthood. Unlike typical savings accounts, the Felicity Junior Fund is managed by experienced professionals who understand market trends and focus on maximizing returns while minimizing risks.

Key Benefits of the Felicity Junior Fund

      Tax-Free Growth: Enjoy tax-free returns, ensuring that every kwacha invested contributes directly to your child's future.

      Transferable Ownership: When your child reaches the age of 16, the fund can be seamlessly transferred into their name, giving them the independence to manage their finances.

      Expert Management: The fund is actively managed by our skilled investment team, who use their market expertise to ensure consistent and competitive growth.

      Long-Term Planning: Provides a structured way to save for future educational needs, big life events, or a solid start to adult life.

      Peace of Mind: With the Felicity Junior Fund, you are securing your child's financial future with a reliable and professionally managed investment.

Why Secure Your Child’s Financial Future?

Investing in your child’s future is not just about providing them with funds; it’s about giving them a head start in life. A strong financial foundation opens doors to better education, career opportunities, and the freedom to pursue their dreams without the stress of financial constraints. With the Felicity Junior Fund, you are preparing them for a financially secure future, empowering them to make informed choices and take charge of their destiny.

Take the first step in securing your child's financial journey with Felicity's Junior Fund—a smart, long-term investment designed to grow alongside them.

Plan Today for a Secure Tomorrow

At Felicity, we know that planning for retirement is crucial for a secure and comfortable future. That's why we've created the Felicity Target Retirement Fund, a specialized investment vehicle tailored to help adults build wealth over a specific period. This fund allows you to choose a maturity period of 5, 10, 15, or 20 years, making it adaptable to your financial needs and retirement timeline. Our goal is to offer you peace of mind and the confidence to enjoy a financially stable retirement.

Why Choose the Felicity Target Retirement Fund?

Planning early for retirement ensures you have the financial freedom to maintain your lifestyle and pursue your passions when you stop working. The Felicity Target Retirement Fund is designed to grow your investments over a fixed period, providing steady and predictable returns that align with your retirement goals. This fund takes the guesswork out of planning by offering professional management and a clear path to your financial future.

Key Benefits of the Felicity Target Retirement Fund

      Flexible Maturity Periods:Choose between 5, 10, 15, or 20-year terms, depending on your retirement plans and financial situation. This flexibility allows you to customize your investment timeline.

      Consistent Growth:The fund is designed to provide steady returns, reducing volatility as you get closer to the maturity date. This stability ensures a secure build-up of funds.

      Professional Management:Managed by a team of seasoned experts who actively monitor market trends and make adjustments to maximize returns while managing risk.

      Security for Your Future:Investing in the Felicity Target Retirement Fund ensures you are financially prepared for your retirement years, allowing you to focus on what matters most.

      Tailored to Your Goals:The fund's strategy evolves over time to prioritize stability and security, particularly as you approach your target retirement date.

Why Secure Your Financial Future with the Felicity Target Retirement Fund?

Retirement should be a time to enjoy the rewards of your hard work, not to worry about financial security. By investing in the Felicity Target Retirement Fund, you’re taking proactive steps to secure a comfortable future. The earlier you begin, the more you benefit from the power of compound growth and professional guidance. Our goal is to help you build a nest egg that supports the retirement lifestyle you envision—whether that means traveling, pursuing new hobbies, or simply enjoying peace of mind.

Choose the Felicity Target Retirement Fund today and take control of your future with an investment designed to grow steadily, providing you with financial stability when you need it most.

Your Path to Financial Freedom

At Felicity, we believe that financial freedom should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their stage in life. Our General Investment Account is a versatile investment solution designed for individuals who want to grow their wealth without limitations. Whether you're new to investing, a seasoned professional, or have already utilized your pension allowance, this account provides the flexibility and opportunity to secure your financial future.

Why Choose the Felicity General Investment Account?

The Felicity General Investment Account is ideal for those seeking a flexible and adaptable investment solution. Unlike restricted accounts, it has no limits on contributions or restrictions tied to retirement plans. This makes it perfect for individuals who have already maxed out their pension allowance or those simply looking for a reliable way to grow their savings. It's an open and accessible pathway to achieve your financial goals, with the freedom to invest as much or as little as you wish.

Key Benefits of the Felicity General Investment Account

      No Contribution Limits:Invest freely without restrictions, allowing you to maximize your potential returns. Perfect for individuals who have exhausted their pension allowances and want additional growth.

      Diverse Investment Options:Access a broad range of asset classes and funds, enabling you to build a diversified portfolio tailored to your financial goals and risk tolerance.

      Financial Freedom:Empower yourself to make independent financial choices, with complete control over how much and when you invest. This account is all about giving you the flexibility you need to manage your wealth.

      Expert Guidance:Our team of financial professionals is here to provide personalized advice and insights, ensuring that your investment strategy aligns with your long-term objectives.

      Transparent Management:Experience full transparency with clear information on fees, fund performance, and investment options, allowing you to make informed decisions.

Why Choose Financial Freedom with the Felicity General Investment Account?

Achieving financial freedom means having the resources and flexibility to live life on your terms. The Felicity General Investment Account is designed to help you take charge of your financial destiny, offering the freedom to invest without constraints. Whether you're building wealth for future goals, supplementing your pension, or simply looking for a flexible investment option, this account empowers you to create the financial future you desire.

Start your journey towards financial independence today with Felicity's General Investment Account—where flexibility meets growth for everyone, no matter where you are on your financial path.
